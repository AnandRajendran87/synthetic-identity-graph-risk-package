# Business Problem

Synthetic identity fraud uses fabricated identities built from a mixture of legitimate and false data. Traditional rule-based systems often fail because the identity may partially validate and may behave normally during early activity.

Key challenges:
- Hidden relationships across accounts and devices
- Reuse of addresses, phones, emails, SSNs, or IPs
- Slow-building credit profiles
- Bust-out fraud after trust is established
- Coordinated fraud rings
- High false positives from static rules
