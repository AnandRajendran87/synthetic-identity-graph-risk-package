# Model Card

## Intended Use
Risk scoring and prioritization for suspected synthetic identity fraud.

## Inputs
Identity attributes, onboarding features, graph features, device features, transaction behavior, and behavioral anomaly indicators.

## Outputs
Synthetic identity fraud probability, risk tier, and investigator reason codes.

## Limitations
The model supports fraud decisioning and investigation prioritization. Human review and institution-specific governance should be used for high-impact decisions.
