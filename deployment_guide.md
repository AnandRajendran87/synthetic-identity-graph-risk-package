# Deployment Guide

1. Prepare identity, account, transaction, and device data.
2. Standardize identity attributes.
3. Build entity graph.
4. Generate graph features.
5. Generate behavioral risk features.
6. Train synthetic identity risk model.
7. Deploy scoring API.
8. Integrate with onboarding and fraud case systems.
9. Monitor drift, precision, recall, and false positives.
10. Maintain governance and audit logs.
